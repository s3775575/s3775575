My Interest In IT

At the moment, my interest in IT includes Web Design although I do have an interest in game development and design. I suppose my interest in IT started when I was given a ‘Speak and Spell’ for Christmas 1991, I remember that I was fascinated with how this toy could help me progress with my spelling and I was intrigued with the way that this toy worked (e.g. how did it know that I spelt a word incorrectly? How did it talk?) Therefore, I wanted to know everything about it.

If there had to be a person who sparked my interest for IT, it would have been my late Father, who always had an ongoing passion for computers and computer software, and until his death in 2013 worked as a desktop publisher. Unfortunately my experience in IT is quite limited, apart for some basic HTML knowledge learnt in High school, and some knowledge of desktop publishing gained when I worked with my Father for a while, I am a total novice

I chose to study Information Technology at the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology through Open Universities Australia, because as I have stated in the previous paragraphs I have always had an interest in IT, however it wasn’t until recently that I thought about seriously studying, or pursuing IT as a career path. I had looked at a few different Universities and weighed up the pros and cons of studying online versus studying on campus, and after careful consideration I decided that the course delivered through RMIT best suited my needs.

During my studies I expect to learn the basic fundamentals of Information Technology and concepts such as web design, programming, coding, app design etc. Basically I want to gain enough knowledge and confidence to be able to apply my skills to whatever project I am working on. So, for example if I were to become a web designer I would want to know how to use HTML and CSS in the correct way to achieve the best possible results.
